Section 508 Compliant Word Documents

An Introduction

What Is Section 508?

Section 508 is a section of the Federal Code of Regulations that requires all public-facing electronic communictions produced by government entities, and many internal electronic communications, to be accessible to people with disabilities.

Why Is it Important?

Ensuring our documents are accessible to all of our audiences is a basic responsibility of being a communicator. Whether our readers are coworkers, customers, or concerned members of the public, we owe it to them to communicate in a format they can use.
