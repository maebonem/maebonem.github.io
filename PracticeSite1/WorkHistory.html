Work History

Vectrus Mission Solutions. Associate Technical Editor, 2016–Present.

As a technical writer and editor for Vectrus, I provide support to the Federal Aviation Administration's Air Traffic Organization, working with a division that performs safety and operational effectiveness assessments of new technologies, policies, and procedures before their implementation in the National Airspace System.

  • Travel with teams assessing the safety and operational effectiveness of new air traffic control technologies, policies, and procedures prior to their implementation in the National Airspace System
  • Ensure consistency and clarity of data collection during assessment conduct
  • Collaborate with Subject Matter Experts to produce and refine correspondence, white papers, assessment reports, and planning documentation

Rowman and Littlefield Publishing Group, LLC. Associate Production Editor, November 2014–March 2016.

As a production editor for Rowman and Littlefield, an academic publishing firm handling primarily scholarly monographs, I worked with authors to shepherd their projects through the production process—that is, the part of the publication process between signing an acquisition contract and shipping completed volumes.

  • Managed production for approximately 15 projects at a time, coordinating copyediting, proofreading, typesetting, and cover creation
  • Managed issues of four academic journals through their review and production process

Conant, LLC. Court Reporting Production Specialist, August 2012–March 2016.

  • Maintained client relationships and provided technical support for Conant's online file repository service
  • Formatted, compiled, printed, and distributed deposition transcripts.
