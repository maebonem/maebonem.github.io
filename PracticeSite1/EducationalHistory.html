Educational History

George Mason University: Master of Arts in English. (In Progress.)

In the Master of Arts in English program at GMU, I continue to hone my reading comprehension, research skills, and writing abilities through courses that span a range of contemporary English studies. In addition, I am pursuing a Concentration in Professional Writing and Rhetoric, which provides specialization in applying rhetorical theory to analyzing audiences and communication approaches for workplace writing applications.

Completed Coursework:

  • Document Design: An introduction to reader-centered design for professional communicators.
  • Technical Editing: A deep dive into improving grammar, clarity, and the overall quality of technical communications.
  • Advanced Proposal Writing: An intensive course on finding grant and contract opportunities, evaluating the strength of the client's position in competing for them, and optimizing the argument for a client's suitability.

Central Michigan University: Bachelor of Science in English. Graduated 2010.

I chose the Bachelor of Science track rather than the Bachelor of Arts because the general education foundation for this program was more well-rounded, providing a better basis for writing in professional spaces focused on a range of different subject matter. My concentration was in World Literature.
